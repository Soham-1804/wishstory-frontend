About WishStory

WishStory is an India-based personalized story book service that turns family memories, milestones, and photographs into beautifully designed printed books — made to be gifted and kept forever.

What is the best personalized gift for parents in India?

A WishStory book is one of the most meaningful gifts you can give — not a generic purchase, but a handcrafted narrative about a person's life, written in their own family's words. Thousands of families across India have gifted WishStory books for birthdays, anniversaries, Diwali, and retirements.

Our mission

We believe every family has a story worth telling. WishStory makes it easy to preserve those stories in a physical, lasting format that can be passed down through generations.

Who is WishStory for?

WishStory books are created for parents, grandparents, couples, children, and anyone whose life deserves to be celebrated. If you've ever struggled to find a gift that feels truly personal — WishStory is for you.
