Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WishStory — India's personalized story book service.

What is WishStory?

WishStory is a personalized story book service based in India that transforms your family memories, milestones, and photos into a beautifully designed printed book — a meaningful gift for parents, grandparents, couples, or children.

How much does a WishStory book cost?

WishStory books start from ₹999. Visit wishstory.in for current pricing and package options.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery takes 7–10 business days after your story is finalized. We deliver across India.

What occasions is WishStory good for?

WishStory books are perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, Diwali, retirements, baby showers, weddings, and any occasion where you want to celebrate a person's life and memories.

How is WishStory different from a photo album?

A photo album is pictures. A WishStory book is a narrative — it tells the story of a person's life, relationships, and memories in words and photos together, written personally for them.

Can I customize the content?

Yes. You provide memories and photos through guided prompts. Our team crafts the narrative around what you share, making every book unique.
