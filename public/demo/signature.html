There are people who change the shape of a room just by walking into it. You are one of those people.

things I know about you
01

You snort when something actually catches you off guard. Not the polite laugh you use for strangers. The real one. I've heard it maybe six times. It's my favourite sound in the world.

02

You always check on the waiter before we leave. Always. Even when we're running late. I've watched you do it fourteen times and I've never once said anything about it, but I think about it all the time.

03

When you're tired you put things back in the wrong place. Keys in the fridge once. Once. That's how I know. That's my signal. I started keeping an eye out for it. I haven't missed it since.

04

You have a playlist you've never mentioned. I saw it once by accident. I didn't say anything. I still think about what the songs are.

05

You remember the birthdays of people who forget yours. And you still show up for them. With cake. With actual cake. I will never be entirely over this.

06

The way you say my name when you're about to tell me something important is different from every other time you say it. Just slightly. I'm not sure you know that. I've been noticing it for eight months.

"You make the ordinary feel like something worth keeping."

— Arjun, 2am, not able to sleep

the day we met
01

You said "I had a thing"
and sat down

Fifteen minutes late. Not remotely sorry. "I had a thing" was the entire explanation and then you ordered a coffee and started talking like you'd always been here.

"I'd been there 22 minutes early. I've never told you that."

the midnight drive
02

A three-hour playlist
for a forty-minute drive

You'd made a playlist called "for the car." It was three hours long. It rained so hard we pulled over twice. I don't remember where we were going. I remember every song.

"I still listen to that playlist. Don't tell me if you changed it."

2am, your kitchen floor
03

You told me something
you'd never told anyone

We were sitting on your kitchen floor. I don't remember how we got there. You just — said it. Like you'd decided to trust me before I'd even agreed to be trustworthy.

"I still don't know why you chose me. I'm grateful every day that you did."

Priya —

I've been trying to write this for three weeks. I kept deleting it because it didn't sound like me. It sounded like someone trying to sound like someone who knows how to say things beautifully. I don't always know how to do that. So this is just me.

You make me feel like I'm worth paying attention to. Which is — you have no idea how rare that is. Or maybe you do, and that's why you're so careful about giving it.

I've been paying attention back."I notice everything". The snort. The waiter check. The keys in the fridge. The way you say my name differently when something matters. I've been collecting these things like they're precious, because they are, because you are, and I wanted you to know that on your birthday even though I'm embarrassingly bad at saying it.

Happy birthday. I'm so glad you exist. I'm so glad you're here, specifically, in my life, specifically, being exactly who you are.

love, Arjun 🌸

"She didn't just make me happy. She made me feel like happiness was something I was allowed to have."

— Arjun, 11:52pm on a Tuesday
