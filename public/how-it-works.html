How WishStory Works

WishStory is a personalized story book service that transforms your family memories, photos, and milestones into a beautifully designed printed book — delivered across India.

Create your personalized story book in 4 steps

Step 1
Answer memory prompts

Fill in guided prompts about the person — their favourite moments, milestones, quirks, and personality. No writing experience needed.

Step 2
Upload your photos

Add photos that bring your memories to life. WishStory books include up to 40 photos across an average of 24 pages.

Step 3
We craft your story

Our team designs and writes your personalized book using your prompts and photos. Each book is reviewed before printing.

Step 4
Delivered to your door

Your printed, bound WishStory book is delivered across India. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, Diwali, retirements, and more.
